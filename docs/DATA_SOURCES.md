# DATA_SOURCES.md — Source Documentation

## Update Cadence
- **Scheme deadlines**: Check monthly. UKRI competitions open/close quarterly.
- **R&D tax rates**: Check annually (April). Last update: April 2024 merged scheme.
- **VC ticket sizes**: Check annually or when a fund raises a new vehicle.
- **ARIA programmes**: Check quarterly. New opportunity spaces announced ~2x/year.
- **Regional stats**: Annual. Primary source: BBB Small Business Finance Markets report.

## Notes
- VC ticket sizes are typical ranges from publicly available fund data, not guaranteed.
- Crowdfunding amounts are net of platform fees (typically ~7%).
- ARIA programme budgets are per aria.org.uk opportunity-space announcements; actual deployment depends on contract awards.
- "Annual funding" figures per region are estimates combining BBB data, devolved-agency reports, and published fund sizes.
